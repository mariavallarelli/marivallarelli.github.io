## 🇮🇹 PyCon Italia 2026

### Answerability-First RAG for Mixed Text and Tables

**Location:** Bologna, Italy

**Topic**

This talk presents an evidence-grounded Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture that validates retrieved information before generating answers, reducing hallucinations and improving answer reliability for multimodal documents containing both text and tables.
