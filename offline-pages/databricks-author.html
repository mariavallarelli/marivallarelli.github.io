            Maria Vallarelli

            Data Scientist, Plenitude

            Follow Maria Vallarelli

            Maria Vallarelli is a Data Scientist at Eni Plenitude who specializes in building end-to-end AI solutions, from multi-modal data pipelines to LLM-based agents. She works with complex time-series and unstructured data and designing scalable, production-ready systems. She is particularly interested in using machine learning to improve renewable energy efficiency and support data-driven decision-making.
