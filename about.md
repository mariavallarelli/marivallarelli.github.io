# Maria Vallarelli

**Data Scientist | Generative AI | LLMOps | Renewable Energy AI**

Maria Vallarelli is a Data Scientist at Eni Plenitude specializing in building end-to-end AI solutions, from multimodal data pipelines to LLM-based agents.

She works with complex time-series and unstructured data, designing scalable, production-ready systems. She is particularly interested in applying machine learning to improve renewable energy efficiency and support data-driven decision-making.

Her work focuses on Generative AI, Retrieval-Augmented Generation, LLMOps, document intelligence, and AI applications for the energy sector.

## Research & Technical Interests

- Generative AI and LLM-based agents
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation and answerability validation
- LLMOps and model evaluation
- Multimodal document intelligence
- Machine learning for renewable energy
- Time-series analysis and forecasting
- Data-driven decision-making
