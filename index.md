# Maria Vallarelli

**Data Scientist | Generative AI | LLMOps | Renewable Energy AI**

Maria Vallarelli is a Data Scientist at Eni Plenitude who specializes in building end-to-end AI solutions, from multimodal data pipelines to LLM-based agents. She works with complex time-series and unstructured data, designing scalable, production-ready systems. She is particularly interested in using machine learning to improve renewable energy efficiency and support data-driven decision-making.

## Projects

### Renewable Energy AI Agents
AI agents for analysing solar and wind maintenance reports and supporting predictive maintenance use cases.

### Answerability-First RAG
RAG architecture focused on validating evidence before generating answers.

### Voice of Customer Analytics
Topic extraction and customer feedback analysis using BERTopic, OpenAI and Databricks.
